Hi, I'm Lily! I’m currently a junior at the University of Colorado Boulder studying Creative Technology and Design Engineering. I bring a unique blend of technical skills and creative passion to everything I do. Beyond my academics, I have a strong passion for water recreation and safety.

I grew up in Santa Monica, CA as a competitive swimmer and loving the beach. I spent every waking moment at the pool or the beach and this taught me the power of water safety and knowledge.

My experience includes being on a competitive swim team from 5 years old to today where I enjoy participating in the CU Boulder’s club swim team. Additionally I have been a lifeguard and swim camp instructor back home in Los Angeles at the Cross Roads School for Arts and Sciences. More recently I taught swim lessons all summer of 2024 at the British Swim School. I have additional experience with kids being a sleep away camp counselor every summer at my child hood camp in Big Bear, CA.

Whether you're looking to improve your swimming skills or need a responsible lifeguard for your event, I'm here to help. Let's make your aquatic experiences safe and enjoyable!
